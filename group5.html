what is Gender Discrimination?

Gender discrimination is defined as any form of exclusion or limitation based on gender that prevents men, women, girls, or boys from realizing, recognizing, or exercising their full and equal rights as human beings.

Gender inequality can harm society as a whole by promoting prejudices and denying people opportunities because of their gender.

By Providing documentaries explaining the problem and how necessary gender discrimination is to be lessened or even better to be stopped and starting a campaign endorsing these solutions through social media.
